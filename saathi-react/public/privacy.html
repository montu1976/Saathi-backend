Saathi Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What Saathi is

Saathi is an emotional support companion for Indian users. It offers AI chat and optional connections to human professionals. Saathi is not a hospital, clinic, or emergency service.

Information we collect

  • Email/password or phone number (WhatsApp login)
  • Optional display name and city
  • Chat messages with Saathi AI and professionals
  • Guest ID on your device if you use guest mode

How we use it

To run your account, save chats, send OTP codes, match you with other opted-in users on similar topics (if you consent), and connect you with professionals you choose. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

Peer-to-peer chats

If you opt in, Saathi may connect you with other online users on similar topics. You can stay anonymous. Saathi is not responsible for any chat, advice, or conduct between two users.

AI & crises

AI replies are not medical, legal, or financial advice. In crisis, contact someone you trust or Kiran Helpline (India): 1800-599-0019.

Your choices

You may use guest mode, log out anytime, or request deletion by emailing support.

Children

Not intended for users under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions or account deletion, email support.saathi@gmail.com.
